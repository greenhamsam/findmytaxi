We're trying to make it easier for regular commuters
to get around Cape Town easily and safely.

Easy directions

Tell us where you are starting from and where you're going, and we'll tell you how to get there using minibus taxis.

Plan your trip

Get an indication of how much the trip will cost you and your likely waiting time: helping you to plan your day.

You already have the app

Rather than downloading a new app, just chat to the bot on Facebook. It's easy to use, just like talking to a normal person. If Facebook is free on your phone, then our app is free to use too!
