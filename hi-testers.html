Howzit, testers!

We care about helping regular commuters get around Cape Town more easily and safely, and we couldn't do this without your help. Thank you so much for giving your time and energy to this project.

What we need you to test

The best way to help us test this app is to use it as a real commuter, which means asking it how to get to the places you're going, and then seeing whether its instructions are actually helpful and make sense. But it's also very useful for us if you run 'simulations' and just pretend that you're using the app.

Here are some things we'd love feedback on:

  • USABILITY: Do you understand how the bot works? Is it clear what you need to do? Did you understand the language we used?
  • STABILITY: Did the bot work as expected, with no weird errors or things timing out?
  • ACCURACY: Did the bot give you good directions, that would actually get you to where you need to go?

We also value your thoughts and ideas about other ways we could improve the bot. Anything that was irritating or confusing, or that we could do better, please let us know.

How to find the bot

  1. Send your name to Sam or Emma so that we can add you as Beta testers. Unless you're a tester, you won't be able to see the page at the moment.
  2. Go to the Find My Taxi Facebook page. It works on web, but we'd prefer you to test on your mobile phone, or however you normally use Facebook when you're out and about.
  3. Like the page.
  4. Click the "Send Message" button underneath the header image.
  5. Tap "Get Started" and follow the instructions.

Alternatively, after liking the page, you can search for "Find My Taxi" in the Messenger app and chat from there.

Known issues

Right now, the biggest issue is that we haven't enabled you to restart the chat. To test the bot again, you need to do this workaround:

  • Go to Facebook Messenger on Web.
  • Find the chat with Find My Taxi on the left. Mouse over it and a settings cog icon will appear. Click it, and then "Delete".
  • Confirm that you want to permanently delete the conversation history.
  • Initate a new chat by searching for Find My Taxi in Messenger or by clicking "Send Message" from the Facebook page, as before.
